# **App Name**: Granizado Go

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Browsing: Allow customers to view all available granizados and other products with images, names, and descriptions, inspired by the 'coctels_ops' Instagram. Includes filtering/sorting.
- Product Customization Options: Enable users to select sizes, flavors, and any available add-ons or modifications for each granizado before adding to cart.
- Dynamic Shopping Cart: Customers can add items to their cart, update quantities, and remove products easily before proceeding to checkout.
- Streamlined Checkout Process: A clear workflow for entering delivery address, contact information, and payment details to place an order.
- Order Confirmation & Status: Display a confirmation screen upon successful order placement and provide a basic status (e.g., 'Order Received').
- AI Flavor Suggester: An AI tool that suggests unique granizado flavor combinations or seasonal mixes to customers based on current popular ingredients.
- Basic Order Dashboard: A simple interface for the business owner to view new incoming orders, including customer details and delivery information.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme to convey freshness and vibrancy, reflecting the fruity nature of granizados.
- Primary color: A bold and lively magenta-pink (#E5196E), reminiscent of tropical berries and exotic fruits.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blush (#F7F0F3) derived from the primary hue, providing a clean and inviting canvas.
- Accent color: A soft yet vibrant purple (#D98FD6), analogous to the primary, used for highlights and interactive elements, adding visual interest without overpowering.
- Headline and body font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for its contemporary, geometric, and precise look, providing excellent readability for product names and descriptions.
- Utilize modern, clean line icons for navigation and product features. Consider integrating playful, food-related emojis where appropriate for product descriptions or categories to enhance engagement.
- Implement a mobile-first, responsive design with clear, accessible navigation. Product listings should use a grid or single-column layout, while checkout and cart pages will offer a guided, linear flow.
- Subtle, fluid animations on 'add to cart' actions, successful order confirmations, and hover effects to provide an engaging and polished user experience without being distracting.
